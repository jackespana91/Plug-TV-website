# paperboy-the-run

## Fish Link Frenzy 🐠

A browser recreation of a fishing-themed social-casino slot, built as a single
self-contained HTML file — no dependencies, no build step.

**Play it:** open [`fish-link-frenzy/index.html`](fish-link-frenzy/index.html)
in any browser (or serve the folder and visit `/fish-link-frenzy/`).

### Features

- **5×4 reels, 50 paylines** with the original paytable (Boat 150 / Tackle 100 /
  Boots 75 / Reel & Gloves 50 / A-K-Q-J 25 for five of a kind)
- **Money fish** (red / pink / blue) land on reels 2–5 with coin values; the
  **Wild fisherman** collects every fish on screen when he lands
- **Collection buckets** — fill a bucket with 10 fish to trigger **FISH LINK**
  with its perk: Extra Rows (8×5 grid), Extra Symbols (double-value fish), or
  Extra Spins (+1 respin and +1 SPIN coins)
- **Golden fish** on reel 1 fills the boat for **SUPER PICK** — 12 cards hiding
  jackpots, coin amounts, and Fish Link entries (up to all 3 perks at once)
- **FISH LINK** hold-and-win: fish stick where they land, spins reset on every
  catch, and the fisherman grows 2×2 → 8×5 on the board — each upgrade awards
  +1 spin and raises his collection multiplier. Fill the whole board for the
  **GRAND** jackpot (unlocked at 360M+ bet)
- Mini / Minor / Major / Grand jackpots, big-win celebrations, autoplay,
  persistent balance, and in-game rules pages (Feature, Super Pick, Fish Link,
  Pays, Paylines)

*For entertainment only — virtual coins, no real money.*
