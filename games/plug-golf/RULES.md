# PLUG GOLF — rules & disclosures

This is the canonical rules text. It is surfaced **in-game** via the ⓘ info panel
(built live from the paytables in `index.html`, so it can't drift) and is the source
for the store description and the compliance items in `stake-engine/SUBMISSION.md`.

## How to play

1. **Pick a club.** Each club is a different risk level — the same 96.5% return, a
   different maximum win and hit frequency.
2. **Aim** by dragging the target on the green, then lock it.
3. **Pull the ball back** to load power and **release** to swing.
4. **Watch the shot.** The closer to the pin it finishes, the bigger the win — up to
   a hole-in-one.

## Clubs (max win · pays on)

| Club | Max win | Pays on |
|---|---|---|
| Wedge | 5× | 87% |
| Short Iron | 10× | 77% |
| Long Iron | 15× | 67% |
| 3 Wood | 25× | 61% |
| Driver | 50× | 54% |
| Sunday Masters | 100× | 39% |

## Fairness (the key disclosure)

> **This is a game of chance.** The outcome of every shot is decided by a random
> number generator the instant you swing, and your bet is settled on that result.
> Your **aim and swing timing change how the shot looks, not whether it wins** —
> they never affect the outcome or the payout.

## Return to player

- **RTP: 96.5%** on every club.
- **Maximum win: 100×** your bet (Sunday Masters).
- Wins are paid as a multiplier of your stake.

## Responsible gaming

Please gamble responsibly. Only stake what you can afford to lose.
